About KeyNee

KeyNee (Connor Kinney) is an independent electronic music producer and DJ based in Southern Arizona. The KeyNee brand represents original electronic releases created and published directly by Connor Kinney.

Independent Production Writing, sound design, arrangement, and final mixes are created in-house under the KeyNee name.
Ownership & Control Releases are independently managed and self-released, supporting long-term catalog ownership.

Professional Statement

This website is the official online presence for KeyNee. It provides consistent brand identification, public access to official streaming profiles, and a professional contact point for business inquiries.
